Who are we...

We are a team of young volunteers from various sectors dedicated to serving all segments of society, particularly in these challenging times. Our mission is to address the daily needs of food, clothing, shelter, and medicine. Despite our own struggles, we remain committed to helping our fellow citizens in the Gaza Strip.
In addition to our core mission of addressing immediate needs, we also prioritize building long-term resilience within our community. Through educational initiatives, skill-building workshops, and advocacy efforts, we aim to empower individuals to create sustainable solutions to the challenges they face. By fostering a sense of unity and collective responsibility, we believe we can create lasting positive change in the Gaza Strip and beyond. Together, we are stronger, and together, we will overcome adversity.

We are currently in a challenging situation, and our primary objective is to alleviate the suffering of people. Our aim is solely to help those in need and we are not associated with any institution that exploits people's necessities. We understand the hardships of not receiving aid ourselves, which is why we are dedicated to reaching out to everyone and offering assistance to the best of our abilities.
In the face of the bitter cold, we are focusing on providing winter essentials to various areas. Despite our limited resources, we strive to raise the morale of children and alleviate their distress by providing them with much-needed warmth and revitalization. We are determined to make a positive impact and offer support to those who are struggling.
